## Sims-Like Game

**What it is:**
A life simulation game where you create a character and live in the resonance network. Your character:
- Has a design (gate/line)
- Makes choices
- Learns skills
- Builds relationships
- Grows over time

**How to play:**

1. **Start the game:**
   ```
   You: "Let's play a game" or "I want to play Sims"
   Agent: 🎮 Starting life simulation...
   ```

2. **Create your character:**
   - Choose your name
   - Set your gate (1-64) and line (1-6)
   - Start playing

3. **Make choices:**
   - 😴 Sleep - Restore energy
   - 💼 Work - Earn money, build logic skills
   - 🎨 Create - Build creativity, increase happiness
   - 👥 Socialize - Build relationships, increase empathy
   - 📚 Learn - Increase intelligence
   - 🧘 Relax - Reduce stress, restore happiness

4. **Watch your stats:**
   - Energy - How tired you are
   - Happiness - How satisfied you are
   - Skills - Creativity, Logic, Empathy, Courage
   - Mood - Changes based on your stats

5. **Progress through days:**
   - Time advances as you make choices
   - Each day brings new events
   - Your character ages and grows
   - Skills improve with practice

**Game mechanics:**
- **Energy** - Decreases with activities, restored by sleep
- **Happiness** - Affected by choices, decreases over time
- **Skills** - Improve based on activities (work → logic, create → creativity, etc.)
- **Money** - Earned through work, spent on activities
- **Relationships** - Build by socializing
- **Mood** - Changes based on energy and happiness (happy, sad, excited, tired, neutral)

**Example gameplay:**
```
Day 1, Morning
- You wake up with 80 energy
- You decide to work
- You earn $100 and improve logic
- Energy drops to 50

Day 1, Afternoon
- You socialize with friends
- Happiness increases, empathy improves
- Energy drops to 35

Day 1, Evening
- You're tired, so you sleep
- Energy restores to 100
- Day 2 begins
```
